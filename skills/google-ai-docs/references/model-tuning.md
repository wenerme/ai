With the deprecation of Gemini 1.5 Flash-001 in May 2025, we no longer have a
model available which supports fine-tuning in the Gemini API or AI Studio, but
it is supported in [Vertex AI](https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/generative-ai/docs/models/gemini-use-supervised-tuning).

We plan to bring fine-tuning support back in the future. We would love to [hear
from you on our developer forum](https://discuss.ai.google.dev/c/gemini-api/4)
if fine-tuning is important to your use case.
