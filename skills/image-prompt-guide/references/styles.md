## Style Detail: Key Styles

### blueprint
- Background: Off-White #FAF8F5, subtle grid overlay
- Colors: Engineering Blue #2563EB, Navy #1E3A5F, Light Blue #BFDBFE, Amber #F59E0B
- Elements: Precise lines, 90-degree angles, dimension indicators, cross-sections
- DO: Grid alignment, consistent line weights, geometric precision
- DON'T: Hand-drawn shapes, decorative flourishes, curved connections

### vector-illustration
- Background: Cream #F5F0E6
- Colors: Coral Red #E07A5F, Mint Green #81B29A, Mustard Yellow #F2CC8F
- Elements: Clean black outlines, geometric icons, no gradients, playful dots/stars
- Style: Flat shapes, bold contrast, high readability

### craft-handmade
- Background: Light cream #FFF8F0, textured paper #F5F0E6
- Colors: Warm pastels, craft paper tones, bold highlights
- Elements: Hand-drawn icons, slightly imperfect lines, layered shadows
- Variants: Hand-drawn (cartoon) or Paper-cutout (layered, torn edges)
- RULE: Strictly hand-drawn, no realistic/photographic elements

### screen-print
- Colors: 2-5 maximum, flat blocks, NO gradients
- Texture: Halftone dots, color misregistration, paper grain
- Composition: Bold silhouettes, geometric framing, 60%+ negative space
- Figures: Silhouettes only, stencil-cut edges
- Typography: Bold condensed sans-serif integrated into composition

### notion
- Background: Pure White or Light Gray #F7F7F7
- Colors: Black lines, one accent color
- Elements: Simple line icons, hand-drawn feel, rounded shapes
- Style: Minimal, clean, approachable, soft
